What is this?

It's effectively coursetable for SOM, a course reviewing website for students to decide on the electives



**Problem**

	• Currently the primary source for students to find course reviews is the som-bidding slack channel. Reviews are random and cluttered there. 
	• There is no good structure to make it easy for current/past students of the courses to voluntarily add reviews.
	• Students also have no way to know how good a review.

**Why?**

	• During every bidding round, the slack channel is cluttered with people asking for reviews on course.
	• Many questions go unanswered.
	• Answers also might not address all components such as workload, individual/team assignments, professor's review, or career utility.
	• Course Review app will allow and incentivize students to add reviews year round, especially when they loved or were frustrated by a course.

**Success**

	• >20% of a class contributing to the app at least once
	• Reviews on 100% of the courses every quarter/semester (depending on the length of the course)
